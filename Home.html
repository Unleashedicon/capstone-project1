"Join the Musical Celebration, Experience Unity!"

HARMONY IN MOTION 2023

Get ready for the musical event of the year, "Harmony in Motion 2023"! This musical extravaganza will bring together the beats of various genres to create a symphony of sound. Join us on the evening of August 20, 2023 , for an unforgettable night of unity and rhythm. As we come together to celebrate the power of music, let's create memories that will last a lifetime

2023.8.20(THU) ~ 21(FRI)
@Grand Park Concert Hall, downtown Los Angeles

Main Program

Local Musicians

The opening act will feature talented up-and-coming musicians from the local area, kicking off the night with their lively and energetic performances.

Guest Artists

This segment of the program will showcase a diverse range of talented artists from various countries, giving the audience a chance to experience a range of different musical styles and sounds.

Collaborative Performance

In this unique segment, guest artists and local musicians will come together to create a collaborative performance that showcases the power of unity and collaboration. This performance is sure to be a highlight of the night.

Dance Intermission

The intermission provides a welcome break for the audience, giving them a chance to get up, stretch their legs, and dance to the beat.

Acoustic Sets

The final segment of the program will feature select artists performing intimate and acoustic sets for the audience. This is a great opportunity for the audience to get up close and personal with the artists and their music.
